History

Welcome to our new garbage collection company in Tallinn, Estonia. We are excited to be a part of the local community and to provide our services to businesses and residents in the area.


Our company was founded with a mission to provide reliable and affordable waste management services to the people of Tallinn. We understand the importance of proper waste disposal and the impact it has on the environment, which is why we are committed to doing our part in creating a sustainable future.


At our company, we believe in reducing our environmental impact, and we are constantly looking for ways to minimize waste and reduce carbon emissions. We work with local authorities and organizations to ensure that we are following the best practices for waste management and to stay up-to-date with the latest industry standards.


We are proud to serve the community of Tallinn and are committed to providing high-quality waste management services to businesses and residents alike. Thank you for considering our company for your waste management needs, and we look forward to working with you to create a cleaner and more sustainable future.
